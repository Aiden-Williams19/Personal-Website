The aim is to develop a website that is visually attractive, easy to navigate, and successful in meeting its goals, whether to share information, sell products, or engage visitors. Web Development

Web development refers to the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves various disciplines, including front-end development, back-end development, and database management. The goal is to create functional, dynamic, and responsive websites that meet user needs and business objectives. Debugging

Debugging is the process of identifying, analyzing, and resolving errors or bugs in software code. It involves using various tools and techniques to detect issues, understand their causes, and implement solutions. The goal of debugging is to ensure that the software runs smoothly, efficiently, and without errors. Effective debugging improves the stability and performance of applications, enhances user experience, and reduces downtime. By meticulously addressing and fixing issues, debugging plays a critical role in software development and maintenance.
